SALTY's Morocco Positioning vs Competitors

Price Position

~USD 2,150–2,650 (EUR 1,950–2,400) for 7 nights. This places SALTY at 2–4x the local Taghazout market. You are NOT competing with EUR 500/week surf camps. You are competing with Soul & Surf, Surf Maroc Amouage, and mid-premium European wellness retreats.

Differentiation

Community + Structured Programming + All-Inclusive + Coaching Staff. Local camps offer surf lessons + accommodation. SALTY offers a CURATED EXPERIENCE with daily programming, professional coaches, yoga, fitness, meals, excursions, and a vetted community. The European buyer needs to see this difference in the first 3 seconds of an ad.

European Advantage

3-hour flight from London. 2.5 hours from Paris. No jet lag. For a European buyer, Morocco is a WEEKEND TRIP distance. This fundamentally changes the purchase calculus — it's not a big-deal international trip, it's an easy holiday.

Regulatory Note

EU Meta ads: no 'negative self-perception' messaging for wellness. Cannot use 'you're burnt out / broken / need fixing' framing. Instead: 'you deserve this / upgrade your life / this exists for you.' Positive aspiration, not pain-point exploitation.
